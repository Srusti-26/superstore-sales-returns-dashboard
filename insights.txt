Insights from Superstore Sales & Returns Analysis

1. Technology contributes the highest profit but also faces the highest return rate (~46%).
2. Sales have grown consistently year-on-year, with 2017 recording the highest sales.
3. Returns are distributed across all regions, with East and Central showing slightly higher counts.
4. Profit lost due to returns is approximately 23.23K, suggesting areas for improvement in product quality and logistics.
